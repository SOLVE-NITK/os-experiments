Theory

Take two process P1 and P2. P1 requires 4 seconds to execute and P2 requires 3 seconds to execute. Let the quantum time be 2 seconds. We start the cycle with P1 as the first process.

  • P1 Process will execute for 2 seconds which is the quantum time and then goes to wait state until its next turn.
  • Now P2 gets the turn.
  • P2 Process will execute for 2 seconds which is the quantum time and goes to wait state until its next turn.
  • P1 Process gets back its turn and it will execute for 2 seconds completing its 4 seconds of execution.
  • P1 Process execution is now complete.
  • P2 Process get the turn and will complete the execution of the remaining 1 second.
  • Hence P1 and P2 completed execution, we terminate the execution.
